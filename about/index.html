Hola!

I am a UK based illustrator from Barcelona who grew up between Spain, Chile, and England. I graduated in 2024 from Graphic Design and Illustration at Liverpool School of Art and Design. I enjoy printmaking, reportage illustration, puppet making and lots of drawing.

Playfulness and curiosity are intrinsic to my practice. I draw daily, my most loved subjects (as of writing this) include - trees, rocks, people, mountains, and things in between. I create with everything, however my most recent fascination is liquid graphite. Visual storytelling is a key aspect of my work. I like to explore how to capture the essence of a moment on paper. Languages and culture are recurring themes in my work. I experience the world through making, and try to create stuff that feels hopeful, and fun.

I have taken part in print fairs such as Lookout. I love meeting members of the public, sharing my work and being part of a creative community. I have greeting cards stocked at Write Here Pens, in Shrewsbury.

I facilitate workshops - please see the workshops section of my website.

Let's chat! I would love to hear about any projects, commissions or ideas you may have.
