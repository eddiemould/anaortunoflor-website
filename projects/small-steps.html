Small Steps, Brighter Future

I took part in the 1.5 Degrees project team, where, through Metal Liverpool we created Sweat the Small Stuff. A creative response to a warming world installed at railway stations between Liverpool and Manchester from Saturday 1 April until Sunday 14 May.
https://metalculture.com/projects/1-5-degrees/

Artwork Description:
As individuals, small changes in our daily lives can add up to a huge impact in our collective response to climate change. Individuals can start anywhere, they don't have to be perfect, just do what you can! Take the stairs, walk, use public transport, try buying second hand, eat seasonal vegetables, choose to shop locally, read, put cold washes on, demand responsibility or vote for environmental action! Any act is better than nothing and can make a difference. A community filled with small actions makes for a brighter future.


Exhibited at Edge Hill and Urmstom Station, 2023
