La Patum

A Reportage Illustration project about the folk festivity La Patum, from Berga, Catalunya, Spain. This project came from my love for Spanish folk, as well as wanting to document and share such a unique tradition. I immersed myself in the festival and documented it on my sketchbook. Afterwards I refined the drawings to make different posters, one of them was shortlisted to represent the festivity in 2023.
